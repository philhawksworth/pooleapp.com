Why use PooleApp?

It is good to keep things simple. Many people are starting to see the benefits of building sites with static site generators like Jekyll or Docpad instead of using databases and server-side scripting. Static sites are faster, cheaper and easier to host, but sometimes you need to mix in a tiny bit of catured data.

PooleApp gives you a form to put on your site which posts to our servers. You can access that data with a simple query either as part of your build, or in the browser with Javascript.

How to use PooleApp

Simply add a form to your site which posts to us and includes your API Key as part of the post. You can include as many text fields as you like. We'll stash it all for you to query later. 


<form action="http://PooleApp.com/data" method="post">
	<input type="hidden" name="api-key" value="API-KEY" />
	<input type="text" name="your-data" />
	...
	<input type="submit" value="submit" />
</form>
